About Us

About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO). It is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization, licensed by the Central Government. It has its base at Chhattisgarh. It holds 80G & 12A certifications, ensuring transparency, accountability and tax-exempt benefits for donors. Our foundation is also CSR-1 registered, allowing us to collaborate with corporate partners for impactful Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives. Additionally, we are NITI Aayog registered, aligning our work with national development goals and hold the prestigious IAF ISO 9001:2015 certification, signifying our commitment to maintaining high-quality standards in operations and service delivery.

Our Projects

InAmigos Foundation Initiatives & Impact

Through a diverse range of community-driven initiatives, InAmigos Foundation works to address some of society’s most pressing challenges. From education and women empowerment to environmental sustainability and animal welfare, our projects are designed to create meaningful, measurable and long-lasting impact across communities.

Humanitarian

Project Seva

Project Seva represents our commitment to humanitarian support and community welfare. Through food distribution drives, clothing donation campaigns and relief initiatives, we strive to ensure that vulnerable individuals and families receive the assistance they need during difficult times.

Impact: Providing essential support, meals and relief resources to underserved communities across multiple regions.

Education

Project Bachpanshala

Project Bachpanshala focuses on creating educational opportunities for underprivileged children. Through learning resources, mentorship programs and educational support initiatives, we help children build confidence and pursue brighter futures.

Impact: Supporting children's education through learning materials, mentorship and community-based educational initiatives.

Animal Welfare

Project Jeev

Project Jeev is dedicated to promoting compassion and care for animals. Through feeding programs, rescue efforts, awareness campaigns and welfare initiatives, the project works toward improving the lives of stray, injured and vulnerable animals.

Impact: Improving animal welfare through rescue support, feeding drives and community awareness programs.

Women Empowerment

Project Udaan

Project Udaan empowers women by providing opportunities for personal growth, skill development and financial independence. The initiative focuses on building confidence, encouraging leadership and supporting women in achieving self-reliance.

Impact: Helping women develop skills, confidence and opportunities for sustainable livelihoods.

Environment

Project Prakriti

Project Prakriti promotes environmental sustainability through plantation drives, awareness campaigns and conservation initiatives. By encouraging eco-friendly practices, we aim to create a greener and healthier future for generations to come.

Impact: Creating environmental awareness and supporting sustainability through community participation.

Skill Development

Project Vikas

Project Vikas focuses on enhancing employability and economic empowerment through skill development programs, training workshops and career-oriented learning opportunities. The initiative equips individuals with practical skills for long-term growth.

Impact: Strengthening livelihoods and career opportunities through skill-building and capacity development initiatives.

Founder's Story

From Compassion To Community Impact

InAmigos Foundation was born from a simple yet powerful belief — meaningful change begins when compassionate individuals come together with a shared purpose. Founded in September 2020 by Govind Shukla, the organization started as a small initiative focused on helping vulnerable communities facing social and economic challenges.

What began as a local effort soon evolved into a nationwide movement driven by volunteers, students, professionals and changemakers who believed in creating a more equitable society. Over the years, InAmigos Foundation expanded its work across education, food security, women empowerment, environmental conservation, animal welfare and community development.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, the foundation mobilized volunteers to provide food, essential supplies, awareness campaigns and emergency support to families affected by lockdowns and economic hardship. These experiences strengthened our commitment to serving humanity during times of crisis.

Today, InAmigos Foundation continues to work toward sustainable social impact by empowering communities, supporting underserved populations and building opportunities for future generations. Every project, volunteer effort and donation contributes to a larger vision — a world where compassion drives progress and no one is left behind.

Govind Shukla Founder & CEO, InAmigos Foundation

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions about InAmigos

What is InAmigos Foundation?

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization based in Chhattisgarh, working across India to serve the underprivileged through food distribution, education, women empowerment, animal welfare, and environmental sustainability.

Is InAmigos Foundation registered and certified?

Yes, InAmigos Foundation holds 80G, 12A, CSR-1, NITI Aayog, and IAF ISO 9001:2015 certifications, ensuring transparency and accountability.

How can I volunteer with InAmigos Foundation?

You can join our community of changemakers by clicking the 'Join Us' button on our website and filling out the volunteer registration form.

Are donations to InAmigos Foundation tax-exempt?

Yes, as an 80G certified NGO, donations made to InAmigos Foundation are eligible for tax deduction benefits under the Income Tax Act.

Where is InAmigos Foundation located?

InAmigos Foundation is headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, but we actively operate and run projects across 28 states in India.

What kind of projects does InAmigos Foundation do?

We run six major flagship projects focused on food distribution, child education, animal welfare, women empowerment, environmental sustainability, and skill development.

Can I intern with InAmigos Foundation?

Yes! We actively offer internship and volunteer opportunities to students and professionals looking to create a real-world social impact and gain leadership experience.

How does InAmigos Foundation empower women?

Through 'Project Udaan', we provide skill development, financial literacy, and livelihood training to help women become self-reliant and financially independent.

Does InAmigos Foundation work for animal welfare?

Absolutely. Under 'Project Jeev', our volunteers rescue, treat, and feed stray and injured animals to ensure they live a life of dignity.

Can corporate organizations partner with InAmigos Foundation?

Yes, we are CSR-1 registered. We welcome corporate partnerships to execute high-impact Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) initiatives across India.

How does the foundation support education?

Our initiative, 'Project Bachpanshala', ensures quality education, study materials, and mentorship for underprivileged children to build bright futures.

How can I contact InAmigos Foundation?

You can reach us at inamigosfoundation@gmail.com, call us at +91 626 730 9902, or visit our headquarters in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh.
